    Our Workflow

    Our process is designed to transform your online organization. A website or application project is a multifaceted combination of art, message, advertising, branding, technology & teamwork.
    Research and Analysis

    Analysis – It all starts with the first plan. We meet with you & your team to get a sense of your goals and ambitions. From there, we begin to formulate an overall idea and direction.

    Research – We identify untapped market opportunities and identify a strategy that will take advantage of them. By getting a deep understanding of your markets, customer motivations, and spreading your competitor strategies and execution, we identify a winning strategy.

    Information Architecture

    In the search engine marketing part we discussed the significance of website information architecture and its effects on website traffic, SEO, as well as customer flow through your website. We design your first website architecture based on what we learned during the research phase.

    Develop Wireframes
    A Wireframes is a visual representation of a user interface. Once we know the exact number of pages/sections & the flow, our UI experts start laying out basic schematics for every page/section, which undergo user testing and build a solid foundation for the future design.
    Design and Coding

    Design – We improve your brand identity, ideas, and values, then use our creative inspiration to create your site design. This process is very collaborative and interactive. We will be in constant contact with you to revise the design according to your ideas and input.
    Coding – The code goes through the process adapting to ensure that the final product renders as planned on a variety of devices, taking into account all the different versions of mobile operating systems and screen sizes. When a special design for mobile devices is designed, it gets coded at this time.

    Testing and Launch

    Testing – Our aim is to build a perfect zero flaws website or app for you. Everything we design and build is included though the battery of tests to ensure that all functional requirements are met, and your website or app renders perfectly on all devices & browsers.
    Launch – During go live, final data & content migrations are handled, hosting is provided, and we deploy your website to the production servers. Once the project goes out to the world, we run though a final test to ensure everything that we designed and built is still working as intended.
