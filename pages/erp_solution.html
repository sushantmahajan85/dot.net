    What is an ERP system?

    An enterprise resource planning (ERP) system is software that helps you automate and improve your core business processes – from finance and manufacturing to procurement and sales. Today’s ERP systems are cloud-based and use technology such as AI, machine learning, chatbots, process mining, and robotic process automation (RPA) to drive digital transformation and productivity.
